How we choose our picks

We do not run our own timed tests. Instead, we rely on three sources: long-term owner reviews from forums and retail sites, feedback from experienced birders who maintain multiple baths, and hands-on assessments published by trusted garden and wildlife experts. We pay special attention to what actually breaks, cracked basins, rusted brackets, wobbly pedestals, and eliminate any model with a pattern of early failure. This approach means a product earns its spot here only after months or years of real-world use, not just a quick unboxing. We also weigh ease of cleaning heavily, because a neglected bath is worse than no bath at all. The three picks above have repeatedly shown they can withstand seasons of weather and regular scrubbing without degrading.
